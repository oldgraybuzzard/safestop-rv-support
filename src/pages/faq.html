Frequently Asked Questions

About SafeStop RV

What is SafeStop RV?

SafeStop RV is a comprehensive mobile application built specifically for RV owners. Version 2.0 is a complete reimagining of the app, adding an AI-powered travel assistant, community-verified safe stopping places, campground discovery, EV charging support, weather intelligence, and a Road Rewards program — on top of the core fuel tracking and route planning tools.

How does SafeStop RV help RV owners?

SafeStop RV provides a full suite of tools for RV travel including:

  • AI-powered route guidance that accounts for height, weight, and length restrictions
  • Community-verified safe overnight stopping places with safety ratings and reviews
  • Multi-day trip planning with photo journals and weather integration
  • Real-time fuel, DEF, and propane tracking with MPG calculations
  • Federal and private campground search and booking
  • EV charging station locations with real-time availability
  • RV maintenance schedules and document storage
  • Weather dashboards, severe weather alerts, and road condition reports
  • Road Rewards points and achievement badges for community contributions

I used v1.0.0 — will my data migrate?

Yes! Your account and all existing data seamlessly migrate to SafeStop RV 2.0. Simply update the app and log in with your existing credentials.

Getting Started

How do I download SafeStop RV?

SafeStop RV 2.0 is available for both iOS and Android:

  • iOS: Download from the Apple App Store (requires iOS 14.0 or later)
  • Android: Download from the Google Play Store

Do I need to create an account?

Basic map browsing is available without an account. Creating a free account unlocks fuel tracking, trip planning, saved locations, community contributions, and Road Rewards points. Premium and Premium+ subscriptions unlock advanced features like document storage and AI-powered recommendations.

AI Travel Assistant

What can the AI Travel Assistant do?

The AI assistant provides instant, RV-specific travel advice. You can chat with it to get route recommendations, ask about RV-safe roads, find safe stopping spots, and get weather-aware planning suggestions tailored to your specific rig dimensions.

How does AI route optimization work?

When you plan a route, the AI analyzes roads for RV safety based on the height, weight, and length you've entered for your vehicle. It also factors in your driving time and time of day to automatically suggest optimal stops.

Safe Stopping Places

What are Safe Stopping Places?

Safe Stopping Places are community-verified locations where RVers can safely park overnight, including rest areas, Walmart locations, Cracker Barrel restaurants, and truck stops. Each location has safety ratings covering noise levels, RV-friendliness, and available amenities.

How do I contribute a review for a safe stop?

After visiting a location, tap the location on the map and select "Add Review." Your contribution earns Road Rewards points and helps fellow RVers make informed decisions. Reviews include ratings for noise, amenities, and overall RV-friendliness.

Fuel, DEF & Propane Tracking

How accurate is the fuel tracking?

Fuel tracking uses your vehicle's specific make, model, tank capacity, and actual fill-up data to calculate real MPG figures across trips. The more fill-ups you log, the more accurate your consumption estimates become.

Does the app track DEF and propane?

Yes. SafeStop RV 2.0 tracks diesel exhaust fluid (DEF) fill-ups and propane usage separately from fuel, giving diesel and propane RV owners a complete picture of their running costs.

Can I use the app offline?

Basic features and previously cached maps are available offline. You can log fuel and maintenance entries without a connection, and they'll sync when you're back online. Real-time fuel prices, AI features, and new route calculations require an internet connection.

Campgrounds & EV Charging

How does campground discovery work?

The app integrates with Recreation.gov for federal campground data and Campflare for private campgrounds. You can filter results by RV site dimensions, hook-up types, and amenities, and read community reviews with real photos.

Does SafeStop RV support electric vehicles and tow vehicles?

Yes. The app can locate EV charging stations along your route, showing real-time availability and connector types. The multi-vehicle support also lets you track both your RV and tow vehicle separately.

Road Rewards

How do I earn Road Rewards points?

You earn points by contributing to the community — submitting fuel prices, writing safe stop reviews, rating campgrounds, and reporting road hazards. Points unlock achievement badges and appear on the community leaderboard.

What can I do with my points?

Points contribute to your rank on the community leaderboard and unlock achievement badges that display on your profile. Additional reward redemption features are coming in future updates.

Technical Support

How do I report an issue?

You can report issues through the app's built-in feedback feature or by using our secure contact form. Our support team will respond to your inquiry as soon as possible.

How often is the app updated?

We release regular updates to improve performance, add features, and fix reported issues. Enable automatic updates in your App Store or Google Play settings to always have the latest version.
