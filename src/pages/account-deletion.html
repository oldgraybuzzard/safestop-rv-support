Account Deletion

SafeStop RV allows you to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your SafeStop RV account directly within the app:

  1. Open the SafeStop RV app
  2. Tap the Settings icon (gear) in the navigation bar
  3. Scroll down to the Account section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision when prompted

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your profile information (name, email, profile photo)
  • Vehicle profiles and settings
  • Trip history and saved trips
  • Fuel stop records and MPG data
  • Campground stays and reviews
  • Community contributions (safe stop reports, ratings)
  • Subscription information
  • App preferences and settings

Data Retention

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Your data will be removed from our systems within:

  • Immediately: Account access is revoked
  • Within 30 days: All personal data is permanently deleted from our servers
  • Backups: Data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 90 days

Alternative: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, you may request account deletion by email:

info@melken-solutions.com

Please include the email address associated with your account. Requests are processed within 7 business days.

Active Subscriptions

If you have an active subscription, deleting your account does not automatically cancel it. To avoid future charges:

  • iOS: Cancel your subscription in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
  • Android: Cancel your subscription in Google Play Store > Subscriptions

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or your data, please contact us at info@melken-solutions.com.
