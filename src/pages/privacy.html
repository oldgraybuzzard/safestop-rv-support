Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 19 Dec 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use the SafeStop RV App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, address, email, phone number, and vehicle information.
  • Location Data: We collect your location to provide RV-specific services such as finding gas stations.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including app performance and error reports.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize app services
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Communicate with you about updates or support
  • Ensure compliance with legal requirements

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information:

  • With third-party service providers to facilitate app functionality (e.g., map providers)
  • If required by law or to protect our legal rights

4. Location Data

We use your location data only to provide app services. You can disable location tracking through your device settings, but this may limit certain features.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of certain data collection or use

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are encouraged to review it regularly.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@melken-solutions.com.
