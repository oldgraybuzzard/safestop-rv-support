Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Melken Solutions ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects information from users of the SafeStop RV mobile application (the "App") for iOS and Android platforms.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address
  • Name (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Authentication credentials (managed securely via Firebase or Apple/Google Sign-In)

1.2 Vehicle Information

  • Vehicle type, make, model, and year
  • Fuel type and tank capacity
  • Vehicle dimensions (height, width, length, weight)
  • Tow vehicle information

1.3 Location Data

  • Current GPS location (with your permission)
  • Trip routes and navigation history
  • Saved favorite locations

1.4 Usage and Activity Data

  • Fuel stop records and prices
  • DEF (Diesel Exhaust Fluid) and propane fill-up records
  • Campground stays and reviews
  • Trip plans and history
  • Maintenance records
  • Safe stop reports and ratings
  • Community contributions
  • Document uploads (Premium+ users)

1.5 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (OS version, device model)
  • App usage analytics and performance data
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Push notification tokens

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core App functionality (navigation, fuel tracking, trip planning)
  • Display nearby fuel stations, campgrounds, and safe stopping places
  • Calculate routes optimized for RV dimensions
  • Provide AI-powered travel recommendations
  • Send push notifications (weather alerts, maintenance reminders, trip updates)
  • Share community fuel prices and safe stop ratings (anonymized)
  • Process subscription payments
  • Improve App performance and features
  • Provide customer support

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, Cloud Firestore database, Analytics, Cloud Messaging (push notifications), Crashlytics
  • Firebase Gemini AI: AI-powered travel assistant and recommendations
  • HERE Technologies: Maps, routing, and location search
  • WeatherAPI: Weather forecasts and alerts
  • Google AdMob: Advertisements (Free tier users only)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: Subscription and payment processing
  • Recreation.gov API: Federal campground information
  • Campflare API: Private campground information

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services listed above
  • Community Features: Anonymized fuel prices, safe stop ratings, and reviews you choose to share
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect legal rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Secure authentication via Firebase Auth
  • Firebase Security Rules to protect database access
  • Regular security updates

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through the App
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of location tracking (may limit features)
  • Disable push notifications
  • Request data export

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time:

  1. Open the App → Settings → Account → Delete Account
  2. Or email: privacy@melken-solutions.com

Upon deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. See our Account Deletion page for details.

7. Subscriptions

Payment processing for Premium and Premium+ subscriptions is handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store credit card or payment details.

8. Children's Privacy

SafeStop RV is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. Upon account deletion:

  • Account access is revoked immediately
  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Encrypted backups are purged within 90 days

10. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through the App or via email.

12. Contact Us

For privacy questions or data requests:

Email: privacy@melken-solutions.com
